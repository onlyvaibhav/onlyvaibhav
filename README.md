## 🚀 Featured Projects

### 🎬 StreamFlix

A Netflix-style streaming platform powered by **Telegram as backend** — lightweight, fast, and surprisingly powerful.
**Tech:** `JavaScript` `HTML5` `CSS3` `Telegram API`

---

### 📵 Unscroll

Take control of your attention. Blocks short-form content (Reels, Shorts, TikTok) after your daily limit.
**Tech:** `Flutter` `Dart` `Kotlin`

---

### 🤖 TriBot

A multi-mode **WiFi-controlled robot** with obstacle avoidance, line following, and app control.
**Tech:** `C++` `Arduino` `Flutter` `IoT`
